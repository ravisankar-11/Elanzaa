ABOUT US

Elanzaa is a national level technical symposium organized by the departments of Computer Science and Business Systems ,Computer Technology, and Information Science and Engineering departments of Bannari Amman Institute of Technology. The symposium aims to provide a platform for students, researchers, and industry experts to come together and exchange knowledge, ideas, and experiences related to various fields of technology. Elanzaa also provides a platform for students to showcase their technical and creative skills through various competitions such as coding challenge, hackathon, competitions, and design contests. Overall, Elanzaa is an excellent platform for students to learn and apply the latest technologies, interact with industry experts, and showcase their talents.
